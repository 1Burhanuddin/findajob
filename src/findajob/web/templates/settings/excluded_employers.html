Excluded employers

Companies on this list are auto-rejected before any LLM scoring — the job goes straight to score 1 with rejection reason excluded_employer. Use this for prior employers, ethical objections, or companies you want to never apply to regardless of role.

Two ways to match: Exact matches the company name case-insensitively (e.g. Apple matches "Apple" and "apple"); Regex matches a pattern (e.g. ^state\s+of\s+\w+$ matches "State of California", "State of Texas", etc.).
