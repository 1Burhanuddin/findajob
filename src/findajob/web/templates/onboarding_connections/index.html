Upload your LinkedIn connections export

findajob can draft outreach to anyone in your network who works at the company a job was posted by. The match happens locally — your connections file stays on this stack, never gets uploaded anywhere, and is never sent to LinkedIn or any third party. This step is optional; skipping just means no outreach drafts get generated until you come back.

How to request your connections export from LinkedIn
  1. Sign into LinkedIn → Settings & Privacy → Data Privacy → Get a copy of your data.
  2. Choose Want something in particular?, tick Connections, and click Request archive. The Connections-only export is normally ready in about 10 minutes; LinkedIn emails you a download link.
  3. Open the email, download the ZIP, and unzip it. Inside you'll find Connections.csv.
  4. Upload that Connections.csv below.

Heads-up: LinkedIn sometimes adds a Notes: preamble at the top of the CSV (a few lines, then a blank line). If you see that, delete those lines first — the column headers (First Name, Last Name, Company, ...) must be on row 1.

Validated locally before save; nothing leaves this stack.

Don't have the file handy, or don't want to enable outreach drafting? Skip this step — you can re-open it via /onboarding/?mode=rerun.
