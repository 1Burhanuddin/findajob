How this works

  1. Open a new chat in your chosen LLM (paid reasoning tier required).
  2. Copy the full interview prompt and paste it into the chat.
  3. Work through the interview (~90 min; you can pause and resume).
  4. When the LLM emits the seven file blocks, copy the entire chat and paste it below.
  5. We'll parse the blocks and inject them into your stack's config.

Before you start

  • Have your current resume (PDF or DOCX) ready to upload into the chat.
  • Optional but helpful: recent performance reviews, 360s, prior cover letters.
  • Pick a paid reasoning-grade LLM (see links below).

Full prework checklist: docs/onboarding-prework-checklist.md
